## Features

* **Sleek PvP Aesthetic**
* **Online Player Count**
* **Regions, Store, Leaderboards, Etc**

## Setup

1. Clone the repo and open `index.html` in your browser
2. Replace the placeholders in `index.html` and `style.css` with your server's name, IP, and details
3. To modify the theme, open `style.css` and adjust your hex colors
